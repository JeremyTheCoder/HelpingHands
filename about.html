Meghan

Meghan has been a military spouse for 13 years. She and her husband, Matt, have three children. She loves being a military spouse and getting to live all over the world. Meghan started cleaning homes professionally in 2018 when her youngest began school and discovered a new passion. One of her favorite things is to bring the organization and order that make a house a home. She loves to help others and add a little sparkle, shine, and care to their world.

Stacey

Stacey has been a military spouse for 16 years. She has been married for almost 17 years to her Elementary sweetheart, Uriah. They have three children. Her favorite part of being a military spouse is all the people she gets to meet and connect with. Stacey's first professional cleaning experience dates back to 2001 when she started cleaning local beach houses in her hometown in North Carolina. She is grateful to be back into the profession after so many years. For her, nothing compares to the gratification of helping others in her community. Knowing that her small contributions and attention to detail can make someones life a little more brighter means the world to her.
